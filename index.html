Chapter 9 — The Visionary

Dr. Heba Hassan

Founder of Education Experts Group (EXG)

ICF-Accredited Parenting Coach Educator MBA Holder DBA Candidate Founder of Heba's Planet

A Journey Built on Education, Leadership, and Impact

For more than two decades, Dr. Heba Hassan has dedicated her career to empowering children, families, educators, and communities through meaningful education.

Her professional journey spans over 20 years across national and international educational institutions, where she developed extensive expertise in teaching, curriculum development, educational leadership, and family empowerment.

Believing that education should inspire curiosity rather than simply deliver information, she founded Heba's Planet, creating innovative educational resources that combine interactive learning, games, and technology to make education engaging for young learners.

One of the defining chapters of her career was leading Kids Palace Kindergarten in Hurghada. Over 10 years of educational leadership, she transformed the kindergarten into one of the city's most respected educational institutions, recognized for its quality, innovation, and child-centered learning environment.

Today, based in Madinaty, Cairo, Dr. Heba continues her mission through Education Experts Group (EXG), helping parents, educators, schools, and individuals unlock their potential through coaching, professional development, and transformational learning experiences.
